School Health Services

School health services refer to need based comprehensive services rendered to pupils, teachers and other personnel in the school to promote and protect their health, prevent and control diseases and maintain their health.

Why Ameyalife School Health

School health services refer to need based comprehensive services rendered to pupils, teachers and other personnel in the school to promote and protect their health, prevent and control diseases and maintain their health.

Prevalence of obesity in school going children has risen drastically in India during the past few years.

Studies have shown that Childhood obesity has been associated with many chronic disorders like type 2 diabetes mellitus, the early-onset metabolic syndrome, subclinical inflammation, dyslipidemia, coronary artery diseases, and adulthood obesity.

High-risk screening and effective health educational programs are the need of the hour.

A recent study on urban Indian school children showed that there was an improvement in behavior & knowledge of about 40-70% among school children after medical education for prevention of obesity & diabetes.

Therefore school health of Ameya Life is a right initiative to opt for tackling this rising issue.

High risk screening & effective health educational programs are the need of the hour to tackle CHILDHOOD OBESITY & prevent disorders like heart disease & diabetes in adulthood. Proper nutritional guidance will be provided to prevent under or over nutrition.

VISION DISORDERS are often not complained of by the student therefore early detection could prevent underperformance in school.

Dental caries & malocclusion are prevalent among school children , proper screening & continuous education about DENTAL HEALTH could help reduce the burden of caries.

Psychological screening to detect LEARNING DISORDERS in childhood & appropriate focus on ADOLESCENT HEALTH issues like substance abuse & changing physical needs would lead to a bright career of the student.

Continuous health education for this age group would lead to a bright career ahead.

Vision

Refractory errors or the need for spectacles is one of the most important causes of poor vision in school children. School pupils often donâ€™t complain about their visual impairment which often leads to underperformance in school. Apart from screening for refractory errors the Ameya school health also screens for squint/crossed eyes, amblyopia/lazy eye which are issues among this age group.

Hearing

Screening for hearing loss among school children by our team could detect the cause which is usually ear wax or middle ear infection or foreign body via appropriate referral.

This could further prevent learning impairment due to prolonged reduced hearing.

Dental health

Dental caries & malocclusion is very prevalent among school children, proper screening & education could help reduce the burden of caries.

Learning Disorders & Adolescent Health

Learning disorders should be detected at an early stage & referred accordingly for further analysis & counseling

Adolescent health needs to be dealt with in a different manner; focus on various adolescent health issues like substances abuse & changing physical needs.

Psychological issues like anxiety, hyperactivity & mood disorders are prevalent and should be screened for.

Continuous health education for this age group would lead to a bright career ahead.

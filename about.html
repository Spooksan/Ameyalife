Mission

Ameya Life is a social enterprise that focuses on school health and corporate, PSU employs health to prevent NCDs or life style diseases

To provide quality healthcare to a diverse population through a variety of service lines Ameya Life has to offer.

Vision

As our name suggests we believe in reaching out to an infinite number of people nationally & internationally. Being boundary less is our vision.

Values

  • Accessibility

    Door step delivery of services& an array of tele services we offer make us extremely accessible.

  • Affordability

    Lean delivery of healthcare is our priority therefore avoiding wasteful expenditure gives our client a satisfactory experience at a lower cost.

  • Accountability

    Our healthcare quality and its timely delivery is our utmost responsibility.

CEO's Desk

Ameya Life is committed to providing first class clinical care and attention through protocol-driven Preventive /Primary care by empowering people for their healthcare needs, enabling physicians to treat patients with the latest evidence based protocol driven care and at appropriate site and at appropriate level, and by enhancing wellness of the individual, his or her family and the community at large.

In this present scenario, there has been a growing awareness the world over of the fundamental importance of the role of Preventive/Primary care. with a constant struggle to achieve a balance between affordable cost and quality care. Changing demography, lifestyle diseases(NCD), ageing population and information technology advances, combined with a much higher level of awareness have played a significant role in health care delivery.

Ameya Life has set out to address the issues in this area of significant need in our health care delivery system, as well as to create a trusted end-to-end provider of Preventive/Primary care for patients.

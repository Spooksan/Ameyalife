Why Ameya Life?

Mobile Schools Health is the only high-impact, mobile intervention that enables under-served children to access preventative healthcare so that they can have the opportunity to succeed in life.

Using large-scale, multi-disciplinary mobile clinics, we deliver optometry, primary healthcare and oral healthcare services directly to schools – ensuring convenience for children as well as their families and schools.
